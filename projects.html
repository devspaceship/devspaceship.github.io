Understanding Artificial Intelligence

Interactive Gridworld

An easy introduction to Markov Decision Processes and Reinforcement Learning by the mean of a gridworld environement where you can play with different parameters of solving algorithms and modify the map to see the effects. We see what are state-value function and policies and we look into four solvers.

Tesseract Projection

Have you ever wondered how the cool rotating tesseract projections are made ? Here I explain what a tesseract is, what does it mean to do a rotation in four dimensions and how to project the tesseract in three dimensions in a stylish way.

Live Happiness Map - coming soon

Using the Twitter API, we estimate a 'happiness' score per country using tweets from all around the world. To calculate this score, we use sentiment analysis methods and try to understand how they works. Once all the countries have an estimate, we visualize this data by coloring a world map in function of this score.
